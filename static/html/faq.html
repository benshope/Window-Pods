INSTALLATION

Window Pods suction cups are specifically designed for long-term installation. Their shape requires thoughtful application compared to conventional suction cups, but, once correctly applied to a window - they form an enduring seal. Here are a few helpful tips:

If the suction cup needs to be cleaned, wash in warm soapy water then dry gently with a lint-free cloth.

Clean the surface you are applying the suction cup to.

To enhance suction, a tiny dab of Vaseline or cooking oil may be rubbed on the rim of the cup.

The suction cup may need to be “burped” periodically by pressing the center to remove any air that may have seeped in.
