INSTALLATION

Window Pods can be mounted on the window or on the wall. Pods have great drainage, so make sure to water your plants regularly.

SUCTION CUPS

Window Pod suction cups are designed specifically for long-term installation. Their shape requires thoughtful application compared to conventional suction cups, but, once correctly applied to a window - they form an enduring seal.

If the suction cup needs to be cleaned, wash in warm soapy water then dry gently with a lint-free cloth. Clean the surface you are applying the suction cup to. To enhance suction, a tiny dab of Vaseline or cooking oil may be rubbed on the rim of the cup. Over time, the suction cup may need to be “burped” by pressing the center to remove any air that may have seeped in.

 With the correct surface prep, The Pods will stick to the window in any condition they encounter. In extreme climates, applying a tiny amount of cooking oil or Vaseline will create an evaporation-proof seal. In our limited testing, pods have remained up for many months with no need to "burp" the air bubble that forms in the suction cup. However, the time a suction cup adheres to a window is affected by many factors - including cleanliness of the window, weight, and vibration. The Window Pods suction cup works around this issue by visibly developing an air bubble over time, which indicates that the suction cup's vacuum is weakening. A quick press, to diminish that air bubble, will completely replenish the suction cup's gripping power. Window Pods look great installed on the window, or on the wall!
