DESIGN

Window Pods are flower pots - totally reimagined for modern, urban apartments. Each Pod is a vertical garden mounted right on your window - where the sun is. Window Pods are modular, easy-to-install, and look amazing on any window.


INNOVATION

Window pods are a vast improvement over conventional planters. By putting plants on the window - they receive the perfect amount of sunlight each day. Not only does this create ideal growing conditions for plants, it also saves space for people in an urban dwelling.

Healthy
