WINDOW PODS

Window Pods are flower pots - totally reimagined for modern, urban apartments. Each Pod is a vertical garden mounted right on your window - where the sun is. Window Pods are modular, easy-to-install, and look amazing on any window.


INNOVATIVE

Window pods are a vast improvement over conventional planters. By putting plants on the window - they receive the perfect amount of sunlight each day. Not only does this create ideal growing conditions for plants, it also saves space for people in an urban dwelling.

Buy Now


Eco-Friendly
Each pot is made of a lightweight, durable, and eco-friendly polypropylene - with an iconic, white minimalist design. The materials used in Window Pods ensure a long-lifespan compared to their manufacturing energy. In addition, homegrown fresh herbs and veggies reduce your environmental footprint (and taste better!)
Convenient
Window Pods are designed to be modular and easy to install. Just press the suction cup onto the window - and slip the pod down onto it. This ease of installation means that you can completely re-configure the layout of window pods in a matter of minutes - allowing you to grid out an urban garden customized to your personal living space. Mount just one window pod, or fill your window with an ever-changing green centerpiece.
